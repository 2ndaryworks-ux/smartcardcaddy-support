FAQ

How do I scan a card?

Open the app, tap the scan button, and center the card in the frame. You can also import from Photos.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Smart Card Caddy processes everything on device for privacy and speed.

Are there limits?

No limits. Scan as many cards as you like.

How much does it cost?

A simple one-time purchase. No subscriptions and no ads.

What if the OCR is wrong?

You can edit any field before saving. If you see repeated issues, email support so we can improve.

Why I Built This

I was drowning in business cards, and typing them into Contacts was a time sink. Other apps wanted subscriptions, showed ads, or sent my contact data to the cloud. So I built Smart Card Caddy: a private, one-time purchase app that keeps every contact organized and ready when you need it.
